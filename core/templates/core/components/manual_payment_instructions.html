Manual Payment Instructions

If you don't receive the M-Pesa prompt or the STK push fails, you can make a manual payment using these steps:

  1. Go to M-Pesa on your phone
  2. Select "Lipa na M-Pesa"
  3. Select "Pay Bill"
  4. Enter Business Number: {{ business_number|default:"625625" }}
  5. Enter Account Number: {{ account_number|default:"01521260661100" }}
  6. Account Name: {{ account_name|default:"Engineering Students Association" }}
  7. Enter Amount: {{ amount|default:payment.amount }} KSh
  8. Enter your M-Pesa PIN
  9. Confirm and send
