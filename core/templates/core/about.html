About ESA-KU

Empowering engineering students through innovation, community, and excellence

Our Mission

The Engineering Student Association at Kenyatta University (ESA-KU) exists to foster a vibrant community of future engineers, providing resources, opportunities, and connections that help students excel academically and professionally.

We aim to bridge the gap between classroom education and industry practice, encouraging innovation, research, and hands-on learning experiences that prepare our members for successful careers in engineering.

Our Vision

To be the premier student engineering association in Kenya, recognized for developing well-rounded engineering professionals who lead innovation, solve complex problems, and contribute positively to society through their technical expertise and leadership.

Our Journey

2013

ESA-KU was founded by a group of passionate engineering students with a vision to enhance the engineering experience.

2016

Launched our first annual Engineering Innovation Challenge, which has since become our signature event.

2019

Established partnerships with leading engineering firms to provide internship opportunities for our members.

2023

Celebrating 10 years of excellence with our largest membership to date and expanding our programs.
